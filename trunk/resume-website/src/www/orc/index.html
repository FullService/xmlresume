Online Résumé Converter (ORC)

Intended Audience

ORC is still in the beta stage and not intended for frequent, repeated use. It is targeted to the following audiences:

  • Newcomers to the XMLRésumé project who want to test out its functionality and get a taste of the great features it has to offer. If this is you, please jump right in!
  • Those who, for whatever reason, cannot install the required software on their own machines. Software installation can be difficult for even seasoned veterans, so if you're having trouble, please use ORC with our full support.
  • XMLRésumé users who are experiencing problems with their own setup and want to troubleshoot.
  • Users of all sorts who want to try out the latest version of XMLRésumé before downloading and upgrading
  • Occasional users of all sorts who won't be using ORC frequently.

Specifically, it is not intended for frequent, repeated use by those who COULD use the XMLRésumé on their own machines but just don't feel like it. Why? The process of filtering and formatting many XMLRésumés can require a fairly large amount of processing power and we want to make sure those who need ORC have access to it

So please, if you like XMLRésumé, please install the necessary software on your own computer. If you use ORC yourself and want to show your appreciation, please consider purchasing an item from the developer's WishList, or contributing to your favorite open source project.

Thanks for your consideration.

Notes

When you upload your XMLRésumé file, you are placing your potentially private information on a very public server. Specifically, your résumé will be viewable by any SourceForge.net developer until it is deleted by our automated cleaning system.

In fact, it is likely that an XMLRésumé developer will quickly skim the build summary and formatted output of your résumé to make sure everything is functioning smoothly. We're not interested in your private life, we're just looking for bugs in our code. (We will never send you spam or use your email address in a way that ticks you off).

All résumés are deleted from the server every week on Sunday around 2 a.m. This means your uploaded files, the formatted output, your email address, and your preferences will be gone. Ka-put.

If any of this is a problem for you, please do not use this system.
