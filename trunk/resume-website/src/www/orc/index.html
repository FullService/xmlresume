Privacy Notice

ORC is still in active development-- this means that it is quite likely that an XMLRésumé developer will quickly skim the build summary and formatted output of your résumé to make sure everything is functioning smoothly. We're not interested in your private life, we're just looking for bugs in our code. (We will never send you spam or use your email address in a way that ticks you off).

All résumés are deleted from the server on the 15 of the month. This means your uploaded files, the formatted output, your email address, and your preferences will be gone. Ka-put.

If any of this is a problem for you, please do not use this system.
