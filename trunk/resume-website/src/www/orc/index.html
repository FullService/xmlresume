Online Résumé Converter (ORC)

What is it?

ORC is a web-based version of the software included in the XMLRésumé library. With ORC, you can format your XMLRésumé without having to install the software or set up Java. However, we strongly encourage regular users to download and install the library themselves-- in the long run, it's easier and more customizable that way.

Usage Notes

Before you upload a resume.xml file, check to make sure the DOCTYPE declaration at the top references either "http://xmlresume.sourceforge.net/dtd/resume.dtd" or "../dtd/resume.dtd"

Examples:

  • <!DOCTYPE resume PUBLIC "-//Sean Kelly//DTD Resume 1.5.2//EN"
    "http://xmlresume.sourceforge.net/dtd/resume.dtd">
  • <!DOCTYPE resume PUBLIC "-//Sean Kelly//DTD Resume 1.5.2//EN"
    "../dtd/resume.dtd">

The part in bold is the most important-- any other value will not work.

Privacy Notice

ORC is still in active development-- this means that it is quite likely that an XMLRésumé developer will quickly skim the build summary and formatted output of your résumé to make sure everything is functioning smoothly. We're not interested in your private life, we're just looking for bugs in our code. (We will never send you spam or use your email address in a bad way).

All résumés are deleted from the server on the 15 of the month. This means your uploaded files, the formatted output, your email address, and your preferences will be gone. Ka-put.

If any of this is a problem for you, please do not use this system.
